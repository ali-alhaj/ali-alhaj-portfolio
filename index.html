About Me

I am a PhD researcher in Computer Science at the University of Hyderabad, specializing in Software-Defined Networking (SDN), programmable data planes (P4), and network security. My research focuses on scalable monitoring, traffic analysis, and secure architectures using programmable networks.

I have published in SCIE-indexed journals (Q1/Q2) and international conferences, with strong expertise in building real-world systems including SDN testbeds, virtualization platforms, and cloud-based infrastructures.

I am actively seeking postdoctoral positions in advanced networking, programmable data planes, and network security.
